Blackjack Rules:


Blackjack is a card game played against the dealer. The goal is to get a hand that totals 21 points or as close as possible without going over.


1. Card Values

    2-10: Face value.

    J, Q, K (Face Cards): 10 points.

    Ace (A): 1 or 11 points, whichever benefits your hand most without busting.


2. Game Start

    Both you and the dealer receive two cards.

    Your cards are face up; the dealer has one face up and one hidden.


3. Your Turn

    You have two main choices:

    Hit: Take another card. You can hit multiple times.

    Stand: Keep your current hand.

    If you go over 21, you automatically lose.


4. Dealer's Turn

    The dealer reveals their hidden card.

    They must hit until their hand totals 17 or more. They cannot hit if they have 17 or more.

    If the dealer goes over 21, you win.


5. Winning

    You win if your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's (without you busting) or if the dealer busts.

    You lose if you go over 21, or if the dealer's hand is closer to 21 than yours.

    Push (Tie) if both of you have the same total.
